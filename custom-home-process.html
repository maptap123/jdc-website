Phase 01

Discovery
& Design

Every JDC home starts with a conversation, not a blueprint. We sit down with you to understand how you actually live — how you use your kitchen in the morning, what your ideal Sunday afternoon looks like, where the kids' homework happens. From that, we build a program that's specific to you.

Our design team then translates your vision into a detailed set of plans and 3D renderings before a dollar is committed to construction. You'll see and approve the home before the first shovel hits the ground.

  • Site visit and evaluation
  • Design consultation
  • Space program & budget alignment
  • Architectural drawings
  • 3D renderings for client review
Phase 02

Selections
& Planning

This is where your home gets its character. You'll work through every selection — cabinets, countertops, tile, fixtures, flooring, trim profiles, paint — with a detailed budget tracker so you always know where you stand.

Nothing goes to contract until every selection is made and every cost is confirmed. No surprises mid-build.

  • Material and finish selections
  • Appliance specifications
  • Lighting and plumbing fixture selections
  • Detailed construction budget
  • Final contract and schedule
Phase 03

Expert
Construction

Once the plans are approved and selections locked, our team begins. We handle permitting, site prep, foundation, framing, mechanicals, and every finish ourselves — no subcontractors you've never met.

You'll have direct access to your project manager throughout the build. Weekly update calls, a live project schedule, and a documented punch list at every phase milestone.

  • Permitting & site preparation
  • Foundation & framing
  • Mechanical rough-ins (electrical, plumbing, HVAC)
  • Insulation & drywall
  • Interior finish work
  • Exterior finish & landscaping
Phase 04

Walkthrough
& Warranty

Before we hand over the keys, we do a thorough final walkthrough together. Every item on the punch list is addressed — not just noted. We don't close until you're satisfied.

All JDC custom homes are backed by a comprehensive labor warranty. We're a phone call away long after the last tool leaves the site.

  • Comprehensive punch list walkthrough
  • Final cleaning and detail work
  • Systems orientation (HVAC, smart home, etc.)
  • Key handover
  • 1-year warranty walkthrough
  • Ongoing support
The Honest Part

What most builders
won't tell you.

Building a custom home takes longer than most people expect — and that's a feature, not a bug. The reason our homes turn out the way they do is that we don't rush selection, planning, or construction to hit an arbitrary deadline.

A typical JDC custom home takes 10 to 14 months from signed contract to keys. Homes with complex sites, specialty materials, or large footprints can take longer. We'll give you a realistic schedule on day one — and we'll stick to it.

Common Questions

You probably have a few.

How much does a custom home cost with JDC?

Every home is different, and we won't give you a number until we understand your program, site, and selections. What we can tell you: we build luxury custom homes in the $600,000–$2M+ range in Southeast Indiana, and our detailed pre-construction budget process means you'll have a firm number before any work begins.

Do I need to own land already?

Not necessarily. We can help you evaluate sites, and we work with several local real estate partners. But we're also happy to work with a site you've already selected.

What makes JDC different from a national or semi-custom builder?

We build one home at a time. While founders Jason and Lisa Cox oversee our rigorous standards, you'll work directly with our dedicated project managers — not a detached sales team. Every decision gets the attention it deserves.
