Our Story

Thirty Years
of Building
Dreams

JDC Construction was founded in 1996 by Jason Cox, a Northern Kentucky University graduate with a degree in Construction Technology. What began as a single-minded commitment to accurate estimating and honest workmanship has grown into one of Southeast Indiana's most trusted names in custom home building and remodeling.

Jason and his wife Lisa share leadership of the company — and together hold more elite designations from the National Association of Home Builders than nearly any other team in the region. They work personally on every project, from the first meeting to the final walkthrough.

We handle everything from focused renovations to complete custom builds, always with the same care and attention to detail — because for us, every home is someone's legacy.
