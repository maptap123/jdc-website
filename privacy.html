Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Our Commitment

JDC Construction ("JDC," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

Website Inquiries. When you fill out our contact or quote form, we collect your name, email, phone number, project details, and any other information you choose to share. This data is submitted through Buildertrend's contact form system and stored there. We use it solely to follow up with you about your project.

Website Analytics. Our website may use basic analytics (such as Vercel Analytics) to understand page views, visitor geography, and referral sources. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual visitors.

QuickBooks Integration. JDC uses an internal application to access our own QuickBooks Online accounting data for job costing and operational visibility. This integration:

  • Reads JDC's own financial records (invoices, estimates, vendor information, and reports)
  • Does not collect, store, or access personal banking credentials
  • Does not share QuickBooks data with third parties
  • Is restricted to read-only access; no financial changes are made through this integration

We Do Not Collect. We do not collect payment card details, Social Security numbers, or sensitive personal identifiers through our website or integrations.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to project inquiries and provide estimates
  • To manage internal job costing and business operations
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Contact form submissions are stored within Buildertrend's secure platform. QuickBooks data access uses OAuth 2.0 authentication and tokens are stored securely. We take reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Buildertrend — contact form hosting and lead management
  • Intuit / QuickBooks Online — accounting and financial data
  • Vercel — website hosting and analytics

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

Your Rights

You may request to review, update, or delete personal information we have about you by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
